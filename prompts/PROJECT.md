- Explain the benefit customers receive
- Make it emotionally engaging and professional

- Introduce the business naturally
- Mention important services
- Build trust and professionalism
- Make it website-ready and customer-focused

3. CTA (CALL-TO-ACTION) SECTIONS

Generate:

A) Contact / Booking CTA
- Encourage customers to contact or book the service
- Make it action-oriented and welcoming

B) Trust-Building CTA
- Build customer confidence and credibility
- Highlight professionalism, quality service, or customer satisfaction

C) Location-Based CTA
- Mention the business location naturally
- Encourage nearby customers to visit

D) Urgency-Based CTA
- Create urgency using limited slots, festive offers, seasonal demand, or high bookings
- Keep it natural and non-aggressive

The generated content must adapt naturally based on the business type.

Friendly Tone
(For salons, cafes, restaurants)
- Use warm, welcoming, and conversational language
- Create emotional connection and comfort

Professional Tone
(For clinics, agencies, coaching centers)
- Use trustworthy, polished, and confident language
- Focus on expertise, reliability, and professionalism

Confident but Simple Language
(For all business types)
- Keep language clear and easy to understand

- Maintain a modern, persuasive, and human-friendly style

- Keep paragraphs short and readable
- Make content emotionally engaging and conversion-focused
- Use clear and persuasive calls-to-action
- Ensure all content is ready for publishing on a real website
- Keep the content adaptable and reusable for multiple local business clients

6. KEY FEATURES YOUR SYSTEM MUST SHOW
